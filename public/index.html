About Us

My name is Valerie Capiral, and I am the owner of Rosie and Company. Together with my wonderful team of experienced and trained dog walkers, we are here to take care of your pups!

We're Owners Too

We're dog owners too, we understand the importance of trusting that your dog is getting the proper amount of daily exercise.

Company History

We have been serving the Toronto area for 5 years, and have grown our business through our reputation for professional service, integrity, and hard work.

Mission Statement

Our goal at Rosie & Company is to keep your dog healthy and happy, and you worry-free!
